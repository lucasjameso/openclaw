Exact handoff for Claude's black-and-gold Excalidraw exports and the Vince avatar lane.

## Output Goal

Ship motion that feels like Forge is telling on itself:
- one painful number
- one clear diagram
- one deadpan line
- one clean fix
- one CTA

## Source Asset Rules

- Export each Excalidraw board to PNG before motion work begins
- Prefer `1080x1920` for short video plates
- Keep Roger audio as the default voice lane
- Use Vince for intro or CTA framing, not full-script heavy lifting unless the render looks believable
- Keep titles short enough to land in under 2 seconds
