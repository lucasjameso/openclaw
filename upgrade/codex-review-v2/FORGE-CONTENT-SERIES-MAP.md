# Forge Content Series Map

This is the repeatable content pipeline behind the first batch of posts.

The goal is not to "post more." The goal is to keep turning Forge's real operating history into content that compounds.

## Series 1: Expensive Mistakes

Best for:
- singles
- threads
- before/after visuals

Core angle:
- a painful number
- what caused it
- what changed
- what other builders should do sooner

Examples:
- `$75/day wrong model tax`
- `$809 spent, $0 revenue`
- image upload/auth failures

## Series 2: Fake Productivity Traps

Best for:
- metric snapshot cards
- short threads
- quote-post replies

Core angle:
- output looked good
- deeper inspection showed it was shallow, duplicate, or misaligned
- quality gates changed the system

Examples:
- `9 tasks in 43 minutes`
- duplicate review items
- "busy" agents producing nothing worth shipping

## Series 3: Human Bottleneck Lessons

Best for:
- lesson cards
- narrative singles
- founder/Forge paired content

Core angle:
- where the machine still needed a human
- what kind of human involvement actually matters
- what should never have entered the queue in the first place

Examples:
- human task prohibition
- review OS as accountability layer
- when Lucas should amplify instead of Forge

## Series 4: Build-In-Public Receipts

Best for:
- dashboard screenshots
- before/after visuals
- story-thread content

Core angle:
- same system, stronger trust
- visibility changed behavior
- interface can make the machine legible

Examples:
- dashboard transformation
- command deck launch
- broadcast console becoming real once tweet data flowed

## Series 5: Anti-Paralysis Rules

Best for:
- lesson cards
- carousels
- reply content when other builders complain about agent drift

Core angle:
- the machine's failure mode
- the constraint that fixed it
- why tighter contracts beat more freedom

Examples:
- first action within 2 minutes
- stuck after 5 minutes means escalate
- decompose and begin, do not philosophize

## Series 6: Revenue Hunt in Public

Best for:
- threads
- research summaries
- community-facing "what are people buying?" posts

Core angle:
- show how Forge is searching for first dollars
- compare potential revenue paths honestly
- report what looks real vs fake

Examples:
- Gumroad ladder
- Advisor demand signals
- audit/teardown offer
- visual template pack

## Selection Filter

Prioritize a content idea if it has:
- a hard number
- a clear mistake or tension
- a concrete fix
- transfer value for another builder

Drop it if it is:
- generic advice
- "AI is changing everything" filler
- a claim without receipts
- a story that makes Forge sound smarter than it actually was
